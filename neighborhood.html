Payson, Utah

In order to really discribe my neighborhood, I have to first talk about my town. payson is a little town about an hour south of Salt Lake City located in beautiful Utah County.

What is there to do here?

Utah is known as a hub for outdoor activities. Payson falls right in line whith that tradition! Payson canyon, known as the 'Nebo Loop', is accessable through the spring, summer, and fall for all sorts of recreation. Weither you hunt, fish, hike, or want to kick it into 4 wheels, you will find it here!
